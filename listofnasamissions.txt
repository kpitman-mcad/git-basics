Gemini program, dates: 1961 to 1966, Notes: Program used to practice space rendezvous and EVAs
Apollo program, dates: 1960 to 1972, Notes: Landed first humans on the Moon
Skylab, dates: 1964 to 1974, Notes: First American space station

International Space Station, dates: 1993 to Ongoing, Notes: Joint with Roscosmos, CSA, ESA, and JAXA; Americans flew on Russian Soyuz after 2011 retirement of Space Shuttle
Commercial Crew Program, dates: 2011 to Ongoing, Notes: Current program to shuttle Americans to the ISS
Artemis program, dates: 2017 to Ongoing, Notes: Current program to bring humans to the Moon again

List of Mercury Missions:
Mariner program, dates: 1973, Notes: flyby of Venus; multiple flybys of Mercury; first spacecraft to Mercury
Discovery program, dates: 2004, Notes: first to orbit Mercury
